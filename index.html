questions?

Do I need to download anything?

+

Nope. Kompis works through Telegram, which you probably already have on your phone. Just click the button, send a message, and start talking.

How much does it cost?

+

The pilot is completely free. After the pilot, we'll offer continued access for 50 SEK/month (about $5) for pilot testers. Normal price will be 99 SEK/month.

What if I'm a complete beginner?

+

Perfect! Kompis adjusts to your level. Whether you know 10 words or 10,000, Kompis will match your Swedish and help you improve from wherever you are.

Can I use text instead of voice?

+

Yes! You can type if you want. But the real value is voice practice — that's what builds speaking confidence and helps with pronunciation.

Will Kompis judge my mistakes?

+

Never. Kompis gently corrects you like a supportive friend, not like a strict teacher. Make the same mistake 100 times — Kompis stays patient and encouraging.

How is this different from Duolingo?

+

Duolingo is great for vocabulary and grammar. Kompis is for actual speaking practice — real conversations, not multiple-choice questions. Use both together for best results.

What happens after the pilot?

+

Pilot testers get special pricing (50 SEK/month) if they want to continue. You'll never be forced to pay — but if Kompis helps you speak Swedish confidently, we think you'll want to keep going.
